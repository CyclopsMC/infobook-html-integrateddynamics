World Item Importer Exporter

Simular to Item Importers and Item Exporters, World Item Importers and World Item Exporters can respectively pick up items from the world into your item network and place items into the world from your item network. Furthermore, these can also be used to interact with dropped items that have inventories, such as Shulker Boxes.
