Networks

Integrated Dynamics networks are the basis of any good automation system. A network consists of a collection of connected Logic Cables and parts can be placed on them. These cables should be managed using a Wrench. Shift+right-clicking a cable or part will remove it, while a regular right-click will disconnect it from other cables.

Even though Logic Cables may look nice, in some cases youâ€™ll probably want to hide them. You can do this using Facades, which can be resemble any material. Furthermore, these cables are also compatible with MC MultiPart, which means that you can hide your cables in more complex facade structures.
