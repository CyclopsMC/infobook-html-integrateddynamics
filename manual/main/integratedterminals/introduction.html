Introduction

Integrated Terminals is an extension to Integrated Dynamics to manage and overview Integrated Dynamics networks.

For instance, the Storage Terminal allows you to interact with your storage networks, such as your attached Energy Batteries. When other mods are installed that adds storage capabilities, such as the item and fluid capabilities from Integrated Tunnels, then you will be able to interact with these as well.
