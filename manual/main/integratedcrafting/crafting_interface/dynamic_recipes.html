Dynamic Recipes

Following the dynamic nature of Integrated Dynamics Variable Cards, recipes that are exposed through Crafting Interfaces can also be fully dynamic. For example, you could expose a recipe that changes based on it being day or night, or the availability of a certain item in the network.

Crafting Interfaces will automatically detect when a recipe is updated, and all new crafting jobs will immediately use the new recipe. However, all running crafting jobs will still use the old recipe.

Note that the more your recipe changes, the more lag may be produced. So use this feature with caution.
