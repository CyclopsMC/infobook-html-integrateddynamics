Menril Trees

You may occasionally encounter tall blue trees. Such trees are called Menril Trees, and have some very interesting properties. First, the leaves give off light. This is caused by the illuminating berries, which are also very tasty! Secondly, the nice-looking wood of the tree contains a lot of resin, which can be used for crafting most of the things in this mod.
