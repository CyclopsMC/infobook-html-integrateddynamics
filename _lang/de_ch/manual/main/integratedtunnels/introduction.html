Introduction

Integrated Tunnels is an extension to Integrated Dynamics to transfer items, fluids and energy over Integrated Dynamics networks.

You can use Integrated Dynamics logic to configure filters, which can be as complex as you desire. "I want to send 3 enchantable pickaxes with a damage value less than 10 to this chest every tick" or "I want to fill this tank until it has 1000mB of oil, but only when my machine is working and if I have less than 10 iron ingots in this chest" No problem! Integrated Tunnels can do that for you, and it will do it efficiently as well!
