Meneglin Biome

Menril Trees are unfortunately quite hard to find. Luckily, there is one biome, the Meneglin Biome in which these trees thrive. The large amount of Menril Trees causes some things in this biome to become blue, which is because of the Menril Resin that is given off by these trees to the environment.
