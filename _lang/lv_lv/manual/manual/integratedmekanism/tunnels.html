Chemical Tunnels

Just like you're used to transferring items, fluids, and energy using Integrated Tunnels, you can transfer chemicals in the same way.

WARNING: The Chemical Tanks from Mekanism do not allow chemical interaction from all sides by default. So you may have to explicitly allow Input/Output for the connected side within the Chemical Tank GUI.

The Chemical Interface exposes chemical tanks to the network, and the Chemical Importer and Chemical Exporter respectively import from and export to them. For more advanced scenarios, you can use the World Chemical Importer, World Chemical Exporter, and Filtering Chemical Interface.
