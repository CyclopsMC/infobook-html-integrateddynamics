Механический Пресс

There will come a time, when you will have had enough of all this jumping on Squeezers. If you haven't automated your Squeezers by then, perhaps it's time to look into making a Mechanical Squeezer. It does not require any manual effort, but it requires energy to run. This also makes it significantly faster than the regular Squeezers and allows more things to be squeezed with a higher yield.
