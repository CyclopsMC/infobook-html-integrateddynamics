Autocrafting

Note: this section only applies if you have an autocrafting mod such as Integrated Crafting installed.

If your network contains crafting recipes, then you will be able to start crafting jobs right from within your Storage Terminal.

All available recipes will show up in their respective tabs and channels. Clicking on a recipe will open a gui to select the amount of times the recipe should be executed. After that, a crafting plan will be shown that lists all required or missing ingredients. After that, the job will be started. All running crafting jobs will be visible in the Crafting Job Terminal.

Power users can shift-click on recipes in the storage terminal to skip the crafting amount gui, and go immediately to the crafting plan overview.
