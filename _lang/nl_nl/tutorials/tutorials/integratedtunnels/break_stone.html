Breek alleen stenen blokken

Make a Variable Card containing a reference to the Stone item value. Place this inside a World Block Importer to break only Stone blocks. Also have a look at the various aspect properties to see if you can manage to silk touch the Stone to avoid receiving just Cobblestone.
