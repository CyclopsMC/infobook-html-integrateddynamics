Energiebatterij

Integrated Dynamics networks can be configured to consume energy. For this, you can attach Energy Batteries to the network to add energy storage capacity for it.

These batteries accept both RF-based and Tesla power.

Applying a redstone signal will enable auto-export mode of energy to all sides.

When this battery is held in your inventory, auto-supply mode can be enabled by shift+right clicking. When enabled, this battery will automatically try to fill the items you are holding in your two hands with energy.
