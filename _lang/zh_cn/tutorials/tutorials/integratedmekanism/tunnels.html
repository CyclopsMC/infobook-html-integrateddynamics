Chemical Tunneling

Now, we're going to try moving some chemicals over our network. For this, we first need to craft a Chemical Interface, and attach it to a Cable so it faces a Chemical Tank.

WARNING: The Chemical Tanks from Mekanism do not allow chemical interaction from all sides by default. So you may have to explicitly allow Input/Output for the connected side within the Chemical Tank GUI.

Next, craft a Chemical Importer and place it on a cable so it faces another Chemical Tank, and import ALL chemicals.

Finally, craft a Chemical Exporter and place it on a cable so it faces another Chemical Tank, and export one specific chemical (e.g. Sodium).

There are multiple ways to create a Chemical value. You can for example create one directly within the Logic Programmer, searching for "Chemical", and dragging and dropping a tank containing a chemical from your inventory or JEI.
