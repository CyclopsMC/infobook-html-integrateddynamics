Introduction

Integrated Crafting is an extension to Integrated Dynamics for autocrafting within Integrated Dynamics networks. Autocrafting can happen with any combination of items, fluids and energy. The two main parts that are required for this are the Crafting Interface and the Crafting Writer.

The Crafting Interface can be placed next to any kind of crafting machine, and can be used to provide crafting options to the network based on Variable Cards containing Recipes.

The Crafting Writer can then be used to automatically start crafting jobs based on the recipes that are exposed by all Crafting Interfaces on the network.
